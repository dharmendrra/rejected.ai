Senior Backend Engineer — Payments Platform

We are hiring a Senior Backend Engineer to own critical services in our payments
platform. You will design and operate high-throughput, low-latency systems where
correctness and reliability are non-negotiable.

Responsibilities:
- Design and own backend services for payment capture, settlement, and reconciliation.
- Ensure exactly-once processing and safe handling of retries and duplicate requests.
- Lead technical design reviews and mentor mid-level engineers.
- Define SLOs, drive incident response, and improve operational maturity.

Required skills:
- Strong distributed systems fundamentals (consistency, idempotency, concurrency).
- Production experience with a major cloud, relational databases, and message queues.
- Track record of operating services at scale (observability, on-call, runbooks).

Preferred skills:
- Payments or fintech domain experience.
- Experience leading small teams and influencing architecture across teams.

Leadership expectations:
- Mentor engineers, lead design reviews, and communicate tradeoffs clearly.

Communication expectations:
- Explain complex technical decisions to both engineers and non-technical stakeholders.
